Some helpful resources for learning banjo

Three Finger

Learn to Play Bluegrass Banjo
I feel like this is a really really great place to start with learning the banjo

Absolute Beginner Banjo Course! // 30 Days of Banjo: Day 1
Personally haven’t checked this series out

Learn to Play Beginning Backup Bluegrass Banjo - Lesson 1
This series is specifically for learning how to play chords and stuff in the background

Clawhammer

Old Time Frailing for the Bluegrass Banjo Player - Lesson 1 #clawhammer

Banjo Blitz Episode 0: Introduction
This series is basically one exercise every video to get better at clawhammer
I personally feel like these are really great for getting your clawhammer technique smooth. One thing is you might want to still check out the other video for is the “bum ditty,” which I don’t think is really mentioned in this series

ozarkbanjo.com/free-beginner-clawhammer-banjo-lessons
Really good for brand new clawhammer players
Only a few of the lessons are free, but they are excellent for getting started!

Single String Basics! // Bluegrass Banjo Lesson

Banjo Lessons (For Experts Only): Single String
This video does make use of music theory ideas like modes and stuff. Which will make this more or less helpful depending on your understanding of theory
